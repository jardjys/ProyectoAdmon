PayBack Period


  • lightbulb_outlineDescription

    Method of evaluating investment opportunities and product development projects on the basis of the time taken to recoup the investment. This period is compared to the required payback period to determine the acceptability of the investment proposal.

    Instructions:
    1. Specify the numbers of periods in a range of 1 to 10.
    2. Put a positive principal in the field.
    3. Put a positive Interets Rate (without '%' sign).
    4. Fill the inflows and outflows in the table bellow.
    5. Scroll down and click 'Calculate'. The Payback period will be colored in green if there is one.
    6. Change the values and click 'Calculate' again to calculate a new payback period.
    You can use 'Clear Data' to reset all the fields but periods quantity.
